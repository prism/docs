# Auto-Rollback

<Callout type='info'>Available in Prism 4.4 and above.</Callout>

Prism can automatically rollback activities when a player is banned.

Disabled by default, this feature will rollback the player's activities for the last three days (configurable) when they are banned.

This **only** works when the player is online at the time of the ban.
