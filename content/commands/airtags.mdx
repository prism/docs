# /pr airtags

See [AirTags](../concepts/airtags).

## Your Airtags

<Command command="airtags" />

Shows an inventory of your airtagged items (requires prism.airtags).

## Player's Airtags

<Command command="airtags view <player>" />

Shows an inventory of the specified player's airtagged items (requires prism.airtags + prism.airtags.others).

## Delete Airtag

<Command command="airtags delete <airtag>" />

Delete the airtag and its associated activity data (requires prism.airtags to delete your own, prism.airtags.others to delete another player's).

## Untag Item

<Command command="airtags untag" />

Remove the airtag from the currently held item (requires prism.airtags). Does not delete activity data.
